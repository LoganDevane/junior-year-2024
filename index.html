About the Team

The New York Jets are a professional American football team based in the New York metropolitan area. They are part of the National Football League (NFL) and play their home games at MetLife Stadium in East Rutherford, New Jersey. The team was established in 1960 and has won one Super Bowl (Super Bowl III) in 1969.
